CLC is a centralized ledger cryptocurrency, powered by brand new chain-of-ownership technology, allowing for a centralized ledger, whilst keeping it safer than any cryptocurrency to this day.

What is CLC?

CLC (Centralized Ledger Cryptocurrency) is a revolutionary and brand-new approach to cryptocurrency! We asked ourselves the most important question: how can we make a currency that is secure, anonymous, and not controlled by a single entity? In response to this, we came up with a never-before-seen approach: we invented chain-of-ownership technology. This is a direct replacement for blockchain. Each coin has a holder and the coin's validity is proven via digital signatures along a chain. Anyone can verify that a coin hasn't been tampered with.

Coin Market Supply

To gradually inject funds into a market in a controlled manner, we use PoW (Proof of Work). Our PoW is based on cryptographic key pair generation: H(PUB_KEY + SEED) < DIFF must be satisfied for a coin to be valid. Private keys stay local. Mined coins prove ownership via digital signatures.

Hard Market Supply Cap

CLC is capped at 50 million. This is mathematically defined: [-x / 1 000 000] + 50
It ensures decreasing issuance and ultimate cap.
